> **大英赛 Task 2 历年真题实战演示（假设考题：大学生睡眠时间逐渐减少）**
> 
> As is clearly revealed in the given material, **college students sleep less than before**. The data indicates a significant trend that **lack of sleep is a serious problem today**.
>

> 
> Taking all these factors into account, we can draw the conclusion that **we must pay attention to our health**. Therefore, effective measures should be taken to **encourage students to go to bed early**.
